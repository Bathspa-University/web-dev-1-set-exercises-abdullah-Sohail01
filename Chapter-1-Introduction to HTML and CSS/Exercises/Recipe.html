White Sauce Pasta

A Creamy and flavourful Pasta made with White Sauce also known as Bechamel Sauce, best for comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 250g of Pasta
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 cup of grated cheese
  • 3 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • Salt according to the taste
  • Black pepper according to the taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

Method

  1. Cook the pasta according the instructions written on the package until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Take a sauce pan and melt the butter over medium heat
  3. Add the flour and whisk it continuously for 3 minutes until the mixture turns golden brown
  4. Slowly add the milk, whisking constantly to to avoid lumps
  5. Cook the sauce for 5-7 until it gets thickened, stirring frequently
  6. Stir in the grated cheese until it melts and well combined
  7. Add salt, black pepper, garlic powder, dried oregano, and dried basil. Adjust all the material according to taste.
  8. Add the pasta you cooked into the saucepan and toss it until the sauce spreads allover the pasta
  9. Serve it hot, garnish it with additional grated cheese and herbs if needed and enjoy the meal
